Title: Dandelions

----

Taxonomical-name: Taraxacum

----

About: Taraxacum, also known as dandelion, is a large genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae. They are native to Eurasia and North and South America, and two species, T. officinale and T. erythrospermum, are found as weeds worldwide. They are also pretty rad, because when you blow on them you get to make a wish.
