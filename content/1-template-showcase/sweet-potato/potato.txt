Title: Sweet Potato

----

History: **The sweet potato, also known as the dessert spud**, was invented in 1923 by a Swiss mathematician that really didn't dig the golden russet that was popular during that time. He decided that since he loved dessert to a greater extant than the average person he would create a starch that would serve as both a side dish and a potential pie.

----

Origin: China

----

Preparation: **The sweet potato needs be unskinned and it is popular to dice **the sweet potato to assure even cooking. Salt & pepper, sugar & spices are typical sweet potato fare.

----

Dishes: 

Popular sweet potato dishes include:
The olive oil slathered, diced and baked sweet potato
Sweet potato pie
Sweet potato fiesta time casserole
Dessert spud hot paste
Cinnamon & sugar & disappointment
